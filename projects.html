Damaskus

Joshua Wilcox, Indigo Nolan and I created this game in 48-hours as part of the Global Game Jam 2026.

This puzzle game, based on the theme "mask", concentrates on a player traversing through levels using different abilities they gain from the different masks they adorn.

I had the pleasure of leading the visual direction, ensuring every asset reflected the unique abilities players gain. I also hand drew all character and environment assets and designed the distinct Mask variations that define the gameplay.
